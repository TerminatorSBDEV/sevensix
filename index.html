SURVIVAL MEMORANDUM

DAY CYCLE (1 min): Preparation stage. The creature stays hidden. Loot spawns in bookshelves & drawers (70% chance of respawn). Search them carefully.

NIGHT CYCLE (5 mins): The Ayuwoki awakens. He always moves toward the closest survivor. Use your flashlight to navigate, but watch your battery.

AWARENESS: The closer you get to the terror radius, the more your screen will glitch. If he spots you, run!

ITEMS: Use bandages, drinks, bats, and cameras from drawers. If blinded, players see light or dark (epileptic). If Ayuwoki is blinded, he is stunned.

PC CONTROLS: WASD to Move, SHIFT to Sprint, MOUSE to aim flashlight. Q to drop active item, E to interact (pick up, search, doors). 1-6 keys to switch slots.

MOBILE CONTROLS: Left Virtual Joystick to move, Right Virtual Joystick to turn/aim. Use dedicated on-screen buttons to Drop, Interact, and Use active items.
