Instructions

GOAL: Survive the eternal dread. Map structures hold materials essential for staying alive. Day is safe; prepare before the Night takes over.

DAY CYCLE (1 min): The Ayuwoki retreats. Storage containers (Drawers/Bookshelves) have a 70% chance to refresh and restock items.

NIGHT CYCLE (5 min): Darkness strikes. The Ayuwoki emerges to hunt down the closest survivor. Close metal doors to buy time; he will violently shake and tear them down!

ITEMS:
1. Bandage: Recover 50 Health. (8s cast)
2. Camera: Flash triggers a 2.5s stun on Ayuwoki. Blinds players. (20s Cooldown)
3. Energy Drink: Restores and locks stamina for 3s. (4s cast)
4. Snickers: Gain 1.5x speed for 10s, +35 stamina, and +20 HP. (1s cast)
5. Baseball Bat: Melee strike stuns Ayuwoki for 5s (breaks on hit). (1.5s Cooldown on miss)
6. Medicine: Restores 2 HP every 0.5s up to 100 HP. (1s cast, breaks on hit)

CONTROLS:
• PC: WASD to move | SHIFT to sprint | MOUSE to turn | E to interact | Q to drop item | Keys 1-6 to select/use slots.
• MOBILE: Use left virtual joystick to move, right joystick to rotate. Use the UI buttons to sprint, drop, use, or interact.

Nearest Entity: The player closest to the Ayuwoki handles the pathfinding and physics calculations, and updates the state. This keeps movement smooth for whoever is interacting directly with the monster, maintaining tension.

Server Hoster: The original host evaluates all map events, and players only render outcomes. This method prevents disputes regarding attacks but may display delayed reactions if the host has high latency.
